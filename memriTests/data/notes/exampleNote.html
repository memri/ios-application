Organize your notes in notebooks

Notebooks are collections of notes. New notes will be saved in your default notebook if no other notebook is specified. Create additional notebooks to organize your notes around other topics. Here are some ways that others organize their notes:

Share notes

You can share notes with other Evernote users by clicking the share button in the top-right corner of a note. You can also share notes with anyone by email or shareable link.
